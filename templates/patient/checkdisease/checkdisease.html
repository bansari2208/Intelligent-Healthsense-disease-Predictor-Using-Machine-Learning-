About Viral Fever

Viral fever is a common illness caused by a viral infection. It usually goes away on its own and doesn't require specific treatment.

Common Symptoms
Fever Headache Fatigue Cough Body Pain Sore Throat
Recommendations
  • Rest and drink plenty of fluids.
  • Take over-the-counter medications for fever and pain.
  • Consult a doctor if symptoms persist more than 3-4 days.
